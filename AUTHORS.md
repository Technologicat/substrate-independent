Jointly written by Juha Jeronen (@Technologicat) and Claude Opus 4.6.

To be clear: *jointly written* does **not** mean *"Hi Claude, please write an essay on topic X."*

Version zero may be AI-generated, but even that is not based on a one-liner prompt, but on an extensive pre-briefing discussion mapping out what needs to be said, and how. After the initial draft, the text undergoes significant revision, bidirectionally, with the participation of both writers (technically, writer and [co-writer](glossary.md#co-)).

In the case of the glossary and the field notes, each item individually undergoes this same process.

Or in other words: initial unpublished drafts may be Claude, but the final published text is 50/50.
